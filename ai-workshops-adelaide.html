AI workshops · Adelaide

Hands-on AI workshops in Adelaide

Bring your laptop and the work that's eating your week. You leave the session with AI tools set up and actually running, not a list of things to try later.

What the workshop actually is

Most AI training is someone talking over a slide deck for an hour while you nod along and forget it by Friday. Mine runs the other way. You tell me what's costing you time, and we build the thing that fixes it together, on your laptop, during the session.

By the time you leave, the tool is working on your real work. That might be drafting content that sounds like your business, prepping for a meeting in two minutes instead of forty, or clearing the admin that quietly eats half your day.

What you'll walk out with

  • Tools set up on your own laptop, configured for your actual work, not a generic demo on a screen.
  • A workflow you can repeat on Monday, whether that's content, meeting prep, follow-ups, or research.
  • The confidence to keep going, because you've already done it once with me sitting next to you.

Who comes along

Small teams, agencies, regional businesses, and corporate groups who know AI could save them hours but haven't worked out where to start. If you can send an email and use Google, you can do this. I'm not teaching anyone to code.

Every person who's walked in nervous has left with a slightly sheepish "oh, that was way easier than I thought."

Where I run them

In person across Adelaide and the wider South Australia, and online for anyone in Australia. If you've got a team and you'd rather I came to your office, we can sort that out too.
