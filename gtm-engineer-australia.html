What I build

Five layers of a go-to-market system

Each one takes a chunk of repetitive work off your plate. You can start with the one that hurts most, or build the full stack over time.

GTM strategy

Deep research on your ICP and market, your positioning, and the channels actually worth your time, so you aim before you fire.

Sales execution

Playbook generation, messaging, and lead qualification, so the team works from one sharp script instead of rewriting the same email from scratch every morning.

Pipeline generation

List building, enrichment and intent signals, and automated prospect research that briefs you on an account before you ever pick up the phone.

Sales ops

Automated CRM updates, pipeline, forecasting and reporting, so the admin keeps itself current in the background while you sell.

The AI layer

Workflows, agents and systems trained on your own playbook, so the output sounds like your business and follows your process, not a generic template.

What's under the hood

This usually means wiring together tools you might already recognise. Claude for the thinking, Clay or Apollo for enrichment, HubSpot for the CRM, Zapier or n8n for the plumbing, and Instantly for sequencing. You don't need to know any of them. That part is my job.
