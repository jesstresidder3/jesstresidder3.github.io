Privacy policy

Last updated 29 June 2026

This website, jesstresidder.com, is run by Jess Tresidder, an AI consultant and GTM engineer based in Adelaide, South Australia. This page explains what happens to the information you send me through the site, and it is written to meet my obligations under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles.

What I collect

The only information I collect is what you choose to send through the contact form: your name, your email address, and whatever you write in the message field. There is no account to create and no login. I do not run advertising trackers or third-party analytics cookies on this site.

How your message is handled

The contact form is processed by FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party service that takes your submission and forwards it to my email inbox. Your details pass through FormSubmit's servers on the way to me, so their handling is covered by their own privacy terms. I use what you send only to read and reply to your enquiry, and to carry out any work we go on to agree.

What I will not do

  • I will not add you to a mailing list or send you marketing unless you have clearly asked me to.
  • I will not sell, rent, or trade your information to anyone.
  • I will not share it beyond what is needed to reply to you or deliver work you have engaged me for.

How long I keep it

Your message stays in my email and project records for as long as it takes to handle your enquiry and any work that follows. After that you are welcome to ask me to delete it.

Your choices

You can ask me what information I hold about you, ask me to correct it, or ask me to delete it. Email me at hello@jesstresidder.com and I will sort it out. If you would rather not use the form at all, you can reach me at the same address directly.

Changes to this policy

If I change how the site collects or uses information, I will update this page and the date at the top.
