An AI automation consultant who builds it for you

Done-for-you AI automations built around your actual workflows. I build it, test it on your data, and hand it over working, and it keeps running after I've gone.

What I mean by automation

Most businesses have a handful of tasks that happen every week, take real time, and follow the same steps every time. Those are the ones worth handing to a machine. The trick is building the automation around how you already work, so it slots in rather than asking everyone to change what they do.

I do the building. You tell me where the time goes, I work out what can be taken off your plate, and I set it up end to end.

What I might build for you

  • A meeting-prep assistant that researches every prospect or client before the call and emails you the brief.
  • Follow-up drafting that writes the next email for you to glance over and send.
  • Lead enrichment that fills in the missing details on a list while you sleep.
  • An on-brand content system that drafts posts in your voice and your colours.
  • A Monday morning brief with your priorities and the prep for the week pulled together in one place.

How it works

Everything is built around your real processes and tested with your data, not a tidy demo that falls over the first time it meets a messy spreadsheet. Once it's working, I hand it over with the how-it-runs explained, and the system keeps going when I leave. No dependency on me to keep the lights on.

Who it's for

Businesses that would rather have the automation built and handed over than sit through training and build it themselves. If you'd prefer to learn the how with your team instead, that's what the workshops are for.
